• Ma'in

    Ma'in Hammamet is located 37 km southwest of Madaba. It is characterized by its waterfalls with hot water, and the site is considered one of the most important therapeutic tourism sites rich in mineral water sources in the Kingdom, which are used in the treatment of many skin diseases, circulatory diseases, joint pain, back and muscles.

  • Mount Nebo (Forge)

    It is located to the west of Madaba and overlooks the Dead Sea and the Jordan Valley. It is believed that the Prophet Moses was buried in this mountain. It is one of the most important tourist and religious sites in the province and is considered one of the official sites for Christian pilgrimage after the visit of His Holiness the Pope at the beginning of the third millennium and the visit of His Holiness the current Pope in 2009, which came to emphasize the sanctity of the religious site.
