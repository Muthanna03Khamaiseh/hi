  • Afra mineral pools

    They are mineral water baths located 26 km north of the city of Tafileh in southern Jordan. The hot water emanates from more than 15 sources or springs, with an average temperature ranging between 45-48 ºC to form torrents and waterfalls that gather in water pools filled with healing minerals. The Afra Baths area includes a center for tourism services and medical clinics for medical tourism purposes. Afra spa water has distinctive healing properties in treating infertility, arteriosclerosis, anemia, rheumatism and many chronic diseases.

  • Barbita baths

    It contains several types of salts and minerals such as: chloride and hydrogen sulfide. It is characterized by a high therapeutic efficacy, and its water stems from carbonate sandstones dating back to the Lower Cretaceous period, and this layer is topped with a layer of gravel in the form of modern sediments.

  • Alsilae castle

    The castle contains palaces, caves, models of art, sculpture and architecture, amazing irrigation systems and watchtowers at a height of approximately 400 meters above sea level, and an area of more than 100 square acres, with one road, ascending in the form of a winding staircase. It faces the fortress connected by a ramp from the village of Sila, which is about ten kilometers from the city of Tafileh.
